## Multi-Location Inventory Synchronization

After months of development, Real-time inventory sync can now be activated across
all business locations. Activating this feature ensures instant stock updates, the
ability to transfer inventory seamlessly, and safeguards against overselling or
stock discrepancies.

<ChangelogImage src="/changelog/v317.webp" />

### New & Improved:

- <Bold>Inventory:</Bold> [Smart Reorder](#) will now enhance your existing
  reorder points rather than replacing them with automated suggestions.
- <Bold>Notifications:</Bold> When you open a notification about low stock, the purchase
  order form will now automatically appear with suggested quantities.
- <Bold>Product Gallery:</Bold> It's now possible to browse through multiple product images in
  the catalog without needing to open separate windows.
- <Bold>Pricing Rules:</Bold> When using an existing product to [create a
  pricing tier](#), users can now choose which customer segment to apply it to, rather than it
  defaulting to the first available tier.

### Fixes:

- <Bold>Dashboard:</Bold> Now, when you move inventory items in a list or
  grid layout grouped by category within the [dashboard](#), the stock levels and location
  assignments will update automatically.
- <Bold>Dashboard:</Bold> The sequence of columns on dashboards organized by
  [sales milestones](#) will now match the order of targets displayed in
  the sidebar.
- <Bold>Mobile App:</Bold> Fixed a glitch that allowed receipt printing
  even in the absence of connected printers or offline mode.
- <Bold>Help Center:</Bold> Enhanced the capability for accessing tutorial
  videos directly from the in-app help center for common tasks.
- <Bold>POS System:</Bold> The customer lookup feature in the checkout screen now respects
  the 'Display full names' privacy setting for staff members.
- <Bold>Reports:</Bold> Resolved an inconsistency where sales reports with
  'date range' filters sometimes delivered incorrect totals.

For more information, refer to our [the documentation](#).

## Advanced Customer Segmentation & Loyalty Programs

The platform introduces the capability to set up intelligent customer segments. These
segments are dynamic, behavior-based groups designed to target
customers with personalized experiences based on their purchase history and engagement
patterns with your business.

<ChangelogImage src="/changelog/v316.webp" />

### Use Segmentation to advance:

- <Bold>Enhanced Engagement:</Bold> Spread marketing campaigns across several
  segments to improve conversion rates for all types of promotions, both seasonal and
  year-round.
- <Bold>Dedicated Loyalty Tiers:</Bold> Assign rewards and exclusive benefits
  to premium segments to preserve relationships with your most valuable
  customers.
- <Bold>Controlled Pricing Access:</Bold> Grant special pricing to specific
  customer groups or wholesale buyers who qualify for volume
  discounts.
- <Bold>Reward Customization:</Bold> Tailor the points earning and redemption rules for
  each loyalty tier according to the specific purchasing behaviors of different
  customer segments.
- <Bold>Automation Features:</Bold> Instantly create and tailor the criteria
  of one or more customer segments for any location or channel, adjusting the qualification
  rules and rewards as needed.

For more information, refer to our docs post on customer loyalty or the customer
segmentation section in [the documentation](#).
